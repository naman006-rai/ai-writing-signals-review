# Sample Input: Humanish

The library board approved the Maple Street branch renovation on March 14 after reviewing three bids and a revised accessibility plan. The lowest bid, from Ardent Construction, was rejected because it did not include the elevator work requested in the January addendum.

In interviews after the vote, two board members said the children's room was the main reason they supported the higher bid. The current room has one accessible table and no direct line of sight from the service desk. The renovation plan adds two study rooms, a lower checkout counter, and a staff workroom behind the circulation area.

The project still needs city council approval because the final cost is $420,000 above the amount set aside in the 2025 capital budget.
